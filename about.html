Lavish Loe

Growing up in an abusive household smack dab in the middle of an impoverished neighborhood, "Lavish Loe" took to writing raps and poetry as an outlet for her inner most thoughts.

Listening to classic tracks from Micheal Jackson to Jay Z , Jadakiss to Lauren Hill, she would tune out the sorrows at home, while also making up her own melodies and metaphors.

Lavish Loe was inspired by these wordsmith(s) where she herself knew that one day her music could become therapy for someone else as it did for her and it will.

By the age of 10, Loe released her very first music video, that’s when she fell in love with the craft of music making. "Intellectual, Entrepreneur, Visionary and A Big Shark."

Over time a few attributes were bestowed upon Lavish Loe. Awakened by a youthful arrest for assault and robbery at the tender age of 16. Loe later accomplished redemption as she was enrolled in college a year later and went on to graduate college at the age of 21. Becoming a veteran of the roller coaster we call life, Lavish Loe has a unique story to tell.

"When I sit back and reflect I am just glad I was able to make my mother proud.

Proving she raised me right and its worth. It’s like my education and my music are my greatest accomplishments."

"Melodic. Metaphoric and Lyrical are descriptions of her music and nostalgic is Lavish Loe brand. Loe understands the responsibility of supply and demand of her generation, she’s a strong believer that real bars matter as well as brain feeding the youth. The reality of drug abuse, gang violence, and struggle are all evident in her tunes; however the vibes of having fun and keeping knowledge relevant are never far behind. Lavish Loe will become a household name, brand and symbol of an evolution that will ultimately set the bar for legendary Music and Sounds.
